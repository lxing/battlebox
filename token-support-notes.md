# Token Support Notes

Token support needed for these decks:

- Pauper: `familiars`, `tron`, `sacrifice`, `faeries`, `cycle-storm`, `inside-out`
- Premodern: `white-weenie`, `black-midrange`, `gw-madness`, `reanimator`, `zombie-infestation`

## Needed Tokens

- `1/1 blue Bird Illusion` with flying
  - `familiars`, `faeries`
  - from `Murmuring Mystic`

- `1/1 black and green Insect` with flying
  - `sacrifice`
  - from `Infestation Sage`

- `X tapped 1/1 green Squirrel`
  - `sacrifice`
  - from `Nested Shambler`

- `2/2 black Zombie` with decayed
  - `sacrifice`
  - from `Rotten Reunion`

- `3/3 green Elephant`
  - `gw-madness`
  - from `Call of the Herd`

- `2/2 black Zombie`
  - `reanimator`, `zombie-infestation`
  - from `Zombie Infestation`

- `1/1 green Saproling`
  - `reanimator`
  - from `Verdant Force`
