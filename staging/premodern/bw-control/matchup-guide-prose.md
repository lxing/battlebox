## Elves

Keep the board clear and avoid falling behind on tempo, their mana engine snowballs quickly. Play around [[Caller of the Claw]], and spare some artifact removal for [[Tangle Wire]] and [[Survival of the Fittest]]. Bring in more graveyard hate if they go for [[Genesis]]. They have no removal, so an unchecked [[Exalted Angel]] can go coast-to-coast.

## Enchantress

[[Engineered Plague]] is a hilarious solution for [[Argothian Enchantress]] and [[Mobilization]]. You need some graveyard hate for [[Replenish]]. [[Dust Bowl]] or [[Vindicate]] their [[Serra's Sanctum]] on sight.

## Goblins

Exterminate all the little vermin. Keep hands that can deal with turn one [[Goblin Lackey]], unless you have a sweeper or [[Engineered Plague]] lined up.

## Gro-a-Tog

Try really hard to strip [[Armageddon]] from their hand, but if you can't manage that, then don't overextend on lands. You don't have a good sideboard for this matchup.

## Landstill

[[Standstill]] and [[Fact or Fiction]] are brutal, try to drain their hand with discard before they come online. Try to keep parity and finish with an [[Exalted Angel]] on their empty board.

## Oath

Shut off their entire artifact/enchantment suite. Keep hands with lots of lands, since you have no boardable defenses against their land destruction.

## RecSur

Break up the graveyard and remove [[Survival of the Fittest]]. [[Recurring Nightmare]] is a pain to deal with, and it's easier to use graveyard hate rather than taxing with [[Aura of Silence]] or trying to catch it with [[Duress]].

## Replenish

Graveyard and enchantment hate, enough said. You must be very proactive, lest [[Parallax Tide]] come down and stomp you. It will probably stomp you.

## Sligh

Pack in the red hate and ride [[Exalted Angel]] to victory. [[Engineered Plague]] is surprisingly versatile, and preemptive against [[Ball Lightning]].

## Stasis

Kill [[Stasis]]. Attack their hand.

## Stiflenought

Stock up as much spot removal for [[Phyrexian Dreadnought]] as possible, overload their defenses.

## TerraGeddon

Respect land destruction, especially [[Armageddon]], and avoid overcommitting to the board. Sadly you're kind of helpless here. Use [[Phyrexian Furnace]] to slowly chip away at [[Terravore]].

## The Rock

Focus on blunting [[Recurring Nightmare]] and the random [[Blastoderm]] kills.

## UG Madness

Cleanse their graveyard of [[Squee|Squee, Goblin Nabob]] and keep [[Survival of the Fittest]] off the table. Your removal is enough to handle the board. Play around [[Daze]].
