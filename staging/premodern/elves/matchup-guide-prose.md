## BW Control

Your biggest bogeymen are [[Infest]], [[Wrath of God]], and [[Engineered Plague]]. Fight these with [[Caller of the Claw]], [[Genesis]], and a solid mana denial plan. [[Biorhythm]] is not as attractive when they can wipe your board repeatedly.

## Enchantress

Load up on enchantment removal and mana suppression. [[Kamahl, Fist of Krosa]] + [[Goblin Sharpshooter]] is a juicy juke against their durdling.

## Goblins

Don't hesitate to trade an elf against [[Goblin Lackey]] openings. Always be ready for [[Goblin Sharpshooter]], and preempt it with [[Elvish Champion]] or rebuild with [[Caller of the Claw]]. Your endgame is wiping them with [[Masticore]].

## Gro-a-Tog

Board against [[Engineered Plague]] and [[Pyroclasm]]. [[Naturalize]] does double duty against their [[Mox Diamond]]. As long as your engine is running undisturbed, your Elves will eventually outsprawl their sparse threats and you'll crush them on mana.

## Landstill

Develop your board quickly to blunt [[Standstill]]. [[Naturalize]] is useless against [[Standstill]] but necessary against [[Humility]].

## Oath

Keep [[Oath of Druids]] off the table. Otherwise, you are quite resilient to their land destruction.

## RecSur

Kill [[Survival of the Fittest]] on sight. [[Shard Phoenix]] is their biggest threat, and they can rebuy it repeatedly.

## Replenish

Attack their mana and enchantments. Go straight for a [[Biorhythm]] kill.

## Sligh

Stay alive. [[Survival of the Fittest]] into [[Genesis]] + [[Wellwisher]] is a good gameplan, albeit slow, and you need to clear out [[Sulfuric Vortex]].

## Stasis

[[Quirion Ranger]] goes nuts here.

## Stiflenought

Keep [[Phyrexian Dreadnought]] off the table and their gameplan collapses. Alternately, humiliate them with +12 [[Wellwisher]] chunks each turn.

## TerraGeddon

Play around [[Armageddon]] and [[Cataclysm]].

## The Rock

Play around [[Pernicious Deed]] and ensure you have answers to [[Engineered Plague]]. Pound for pound on board presence, you outvalue them quite heavily, but keeping it around is tricky.

## UG Madness

No sweat, you beat the crap out of them. Watch out for [[Cave-In]].
