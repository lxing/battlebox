## Affinity

They will lean on [[Krark-Clan Shaman]], avoid overextension.

## Bogles

You are one of the few decks capable of outscaling Bogles in the late game with [[Nyxborn Hydra]], [[Timberwatch Elf]], and [[Wellwisher]]. No need to overthink things, just play your gameplan. However, if they do switch to Infect, you'll need to bring in [[Cast Down]] and race more aggressively.

## Broodscale

Overrun them, sprinkling in some disruption to stop the combo.

## Caw-Gates

They will bring in [[Breath Weapon]], counter with [[Hallow]].

## Delver

[[Wellwisher]] will help you race and [[Cast Down]] is good solid removal to clear a few serpents. You are favored; absorb their pressure and apply your own with [[Avenging Hunter]] if the coast is clear of fliers.

## Ephemerate

[[Hallow]] vs [[Breath Weapon]] and their mountain of burn. [[Cast Down]] can slightly disrupt some [[Ephemerate]] chains. Watch out for [[Standard Bearer]].

## Gardens

Lots of removal will prevent you from hitting critical mass. Use [[Cast Down]] to try and catch a turn 3 [[Crypt Rats]] before they can pop it, or to get rid of a [[Writhing Chrysalis]]. You can claw back some card advantage with [[Winding Way]], but they will definitely outdraw you.

## Glintblade

Play your normal gameplan, but [[Suffocating Fumes]] and [[Arms of Hadar]] will make life difficult for you. Best to catch it in the bud. Use [[Nyxborn Hydra]] to scale out of reach and also block their fliers.

## Madness Burn

Lean hard on lifegain; [[Essence Warden]] and [[Hallow]] swing the race in your favor. They will bring in [[End the Festivities]], which [[Hallow]] will do double duty against. Your priority is to get a [[Wellwisher]] with a [[Nyxborn Hydra]] attached for durability.

## Ramp

They will create a huge board and so will you. Counteract their sweepers with [[Hallow]] and try to outscale before getting wiped.

## Skred

[[Hallow]] as boardwipe defense. They have a lot of burn, pace yourself and don't get demorazlied.

## Synthesizer

Spam lifegain. Games are so quick that [[Nyxborn Hydra]] is mostly overkill.

## Turbo Fog

This is very tough. Pursue early aggression. Failing that, build up a huge board and use multiple [[Mesmeric Fiend]] to try and strip them of protection for an instant kill.

## White Weenie

Neutralize pressure with lots of lifegain, and play reach creatures to stabilize the air. [[Standard Bearer]] is a nuisance against [[Nyxborn Hydra]] and [[Timberwatch Elf]] so you'll need to remove it. Play around [[Prismatic Strands]], especially when you think you have the win.
