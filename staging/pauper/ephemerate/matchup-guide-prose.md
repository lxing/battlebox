## Affinity

Bring in artifact removal and surgically deal with their most threatening pieces. The biggest risk is they out-attrition you, so trim their graveyard and keep parity with your own value engine. They only have one basic land, so [[Cleansing Wildfire]] can do a number on them.

## Bogles

[[Standard Bearer]] steals their auras, then [[Dawnbringer Cleric]] goes nuts with [[Ephemerate]]. [[Stonehorn Dignitary]] completes the trifecta of humiliation. No fear.

## Broodscale

Bring removal suited for their combo pieces, though you are already quite removal-heavy. [[Standard Bearer]] will disrupt them until they can deal with her.

## Caw-Gates

[[Pyroblast]] their card draw and counters. Sweep repeatedly with [[Breath Weapon]], and use [[Standard Bearer]] and [[Cleansing Wildfire]] to neutralize [[Basilisk Gate]]. [[Stonehorn Dignitary]] can soft-wall them but is vulnerable to [[Thraben Charm]].

## Delver

Remove or counter their main threats and try to pull ahead in the long game. Protect you [[Ephemerate]]s with countermagic.

## Elves

Sweep and spray removal everywhere. [[Standard Bearer]] blunts [[Nyxborn Hydra]] and [[Timberwatch Elf]]. [[Stonehorn Dignitary]] completely ****s them.

## Gardens

Their removal is quite tough on your [[Ephemerate]] chains. Try to stick a board presence, and don't fall behind on Monarch / Initiative if possible. [[Writhing Chrysalis]] is unbelievably stinky and walls your entire deck. Kill or counter before it can grow out of control.

## Glintblade

This is an attrition match and both decks are roughly equally equipped. [[Breath Weapon]] and [[Murmuring Mystic]] are qutie effective. Avoid being blown out by [[Cast Down]] when you go for [[Ephemerate]].

## Madness Burn

But time with [[Blue Elemental Blast]] and lifegain while until the value loop takes over.

## Ramp

This matchup is tricky, they outscale you quickly in the midgame and you'll be playing catchup against the Initiative. [[Writhing Chrysalis]] also walls you. Target [[Arbor Elf]] immediately and defend with [[Counterspell]]. In postboard games, target an endgame of [[Stonehorn Dignitary]] plus [[Murmuring Mystic]].

## Skred

Go toe-to-toe with them on blast spam. Things will quickly degenerate into blast wars.

## Synthesizer

Pack in as much lifegain as possible. Trim the board to nip [[Goblin Bushwhacker]] bursts in the bud.

## Turbo Fog

You're painfully slow, so you'll need to accumulate countermagic and counter your way through fogs. [[Ephemerate]] and [[Archaeomancer]] can give you multiple attempts at a killing blow and lots of card advantage. Disrupt their light recursion with [[Dawnbringer Cleric]] - not great, but it's somethingj.

## White Weenie

Cull their board before they grow too wide and line up a lethal [[Guardians' Pledge]]. Play carefully around [[Prismatic Strands]] while stripping recursive elements from their graveyard with [[Dawnbringer Cleric]] and [[Thraben Charm]]. [[Stonehorn Dignitary]] and [[Murmuring Mystic]] make for a great soft lock against their combat-centric gameplan.
