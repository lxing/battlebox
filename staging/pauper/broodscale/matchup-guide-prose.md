## Affinity

They will side in extra removal you need to neutralize. [[Makeshift Munitions]] and [[Krark-Clan Shaman]] are very dangerous, you'll need to ensure you have enough spare mana to scale out of their repeated damage. [[Drown in Sorrow]] is pretty good for clearing out their small blockers once you assemble the combo.

## Bogles

Circle will shutdown one third of their creatures, bypassing Hexproof. If they pivot to the Infect plan, consider bringing in [[Snuff Out]] instead of [[Mesmeric Fiend]].

## Caw-Gates

Disrupt their [[Thraben Charm]], wipe the board repeatedly. Combo in a single turn or else risk exposing yourself to [[Dawnbringer Cleric]] and [[Journey to Nowhere]].

## Delver

[[Circle of Protection: Blue]] screws them. Beware [[Annul]] on top of their additional countermagic.

## Elves

Sweep the board repeatedly. They will bring in [[Cast Down]] so you cannot combo with complete impunity.

## Ephemerate

Disrupt their value chain with [[Snuff Out]]. This is still resource negative against ETB effects, so focus on the combo. [[Standard Bearer]] is must-kill.

## Gardens

Fight through their avalanche of removal.

## Glintblade

Sweep aggressively. They will bring in enchantment removal and try to [[Okiba-Gang Shinobi]] your ass. Don't extend into [[Tithing Blade / Consuming Sepulcher]].

## Madness Burn

Keep your life total above water. [[Trespasser's Curse]] is incredibly dangerous and will force you to [[Duress]] it or win with [[Nadier's Nightblade]] on the board.

## Ramp

[[Deglamer]] is their only defense against the combo. You're in good shape as long as you don't get overrun.

## Skred

[[Circle of Protection: Blue]] only buys good time against [[Tolarian Terror]], you're better off fighting their removal and countermagic.

## Synthesizer

Just stay alive. Trim the more durdly deck pieces.

## Turbo Fog

[[Nadier's Nightblade]] wrecks their fog plan. Play around countermagic.

## White Weenie

Sweep away. Beware, they have a ton of enchantment removal, and [[Standard Bearer]] will stop your combo.
