## Bogles

You are mostly racing. [[Toxin Analysis]] and [[Krark-Clan Shaman]] already do great work against hexproof creatures. Keep the [[Cast Down]] to fight [[Crimson Acolyte]].

## Broodscale

Extra removal for [[Basking Broodscale]].

## Caw-Gates

Standard blast package. Spot removal like [[Cast Down]] is considerably less useful. [[Cast into the Fire]] is a more reliable 2-for-1 and good against the [[Gorilla Shaman]] they'll inevitably bring in.

## Delver

Blast away. Careful of counters on [[Deadly Dispute]] and [[Reckoner's Bargain]].

## Elves

Extra removal to keep them from snowballing. You still need [[Toxin Analysis]] to break down their bigger threats featuring [[Nyxborn Hydra]] and [[Timberwatch Elf]].

## Ephemerate

Blasts fight their blue creature base.

## Gardens

Brutal attrition fight between two of the grindiest decks in the metagame. [[Kenku Artificer]] is golden, try to keep their board state in check to avoid getting blown out by Initiative or Monarch.

## Glintblade

Punish their artifact lands and Clue tokens.

## Madness Burn

Race with [[Toxin Analysis]] and [[Reckoner's Bargain]] + [[Myr Enforcer]]. [[Gorilla Shaman]] is pretty good against their Blood tokens and artifact land base, but don't get carried away while they burn your face off. Need more removal to fight their own Gorillas.

## Ramp

[[Cast Down]] and [[Krark-Clan Shaman]] combo is your cleanest answer to their fatties. Kill [[Arbor Elf]] and [[Gorilla Shaman]] on sight.

## Skred

Blast away.

## Synthesizer

[[Krark-Clan Shaman]] and [[Toxin Analysis]] are super juicy against a wide board.

## Turbo Fog

Use blasts to fight their card draw and counters. Churn through your deck and try to kill with protected [[Galvanic Blast]] and [[Makeshift Munitions]].

## White Weenie

Mow down the board with [[Krark-Clan Shaman]].
