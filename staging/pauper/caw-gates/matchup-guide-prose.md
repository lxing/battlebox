## Affinity

[[Dust to Dust]] and [[Gorilla Shaman]] completely wreck their manabase. Their recursion value engine is quite strong, but your Lifelink plan scales even better into the long game.

## Bogles

[[Breath Weapon]] can wipe out creatures without enchantments, whereas spot removal is useless. If they use [[Blighted Agent]], consider bringing [[Journey to Nowhere]] back in.

## Broodscale

Difficult. Counter their disruption and try to nail them with [[Thraben Charm]]. You can block and remove [[Basking Broodscale]] or [[Sadistic Glee]] the next turn, but this is not useful against [[Nadier's Nightblade]].

## Delver

You have many trumps. [[Sacred Cat]] with enough gates outraces serpents. [[The Modern Age / Vector Glider]] trades cleanly against [[Delver of Secrets / Insectile Aberration]]. Trim their graveyard proactively.

## Elves

[[Breath Weapon]] all over these dorks. Your enchantment removal serves double duty against [[Nyxborn Hydra]].

## Ephemerate

[[Pyroblast]] their creatures, especially [[Murmuring Mystic]]. They have a heavy artifact land base, but [[Dust to Dust]] is probably not worth it. [[Standard Bearer]] is annoying and you will need to save removal for it.

## Gardens

This will be a slow knife fight. They have tons of fodder and so do you. [[Squadron Hawk]] will help you win the Monarch / Initiative.

## Glintblade

[[Dust to Dust]] and [[Gorilla Shaman]] punish their artifact base heavily.

## Madness Burn

Blasts trade for burn spells, and [[Breath Weapon]] hits their whole board. Your lifegain and [[Prismatic Strands]] will give them fits. Don't overextend into [[End the Festivities]] (or your own [[Breath Weapon]]).

## Ramp

This matchup is abysmal. [[Writhing Chrysalis]] dominates [[Guardian of the Guildpact]] and their entire top-end has reach. Pray you can kill their mana dorks, counter their most dangerous plays, and disenchant their ramp.

## Skred

Use the blast package to fight their burn and counters while you assemble Basilisk Gate.

## Synthesizer

Standard red protocol. You have plenty of lifegain and [[Prismatic Strands]].

## Turbo Fog

Difficult because you are quite slow. Build up an alpha strike and counter their fog effect. Fight the countermagic with [[Pyroblast]]. Alternately, deck them by breaking their [[Stream of Thought]]; this will be a miserable experience.

## White Weenie

Standard [[Breath Weapon]] sweeping. You will be trading [[Prismatic Strands]] strikes against each other, but you have a small edge due to countermagic and graveyard hate. Beware [[Guardians' Pledge]] which can kill you out of nowhere.
