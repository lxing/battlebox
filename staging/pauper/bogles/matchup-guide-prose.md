## Affinity

[[Crimson Acolyte]] blanks [[Krark-Clan Shaman]].

## Broodscale

Go for pure speed with the Infect plan. Beware [[Circle of Protection: Blue]], which will neutralize your [[Slippery Bogle]] and [[Blighted Agent]].

## Caw-Gates

They'll likely side out removal, and they have a zillion blockers and big lifelink creatures, so try to juke with [[Blighted Agent]]. [[Gladecover Scout]] gets walled by [[Guardian of the Guildpact]].

## Delver

They'll aggressively go for [[Delver|Delver of Secrets / Insectile Aberration]] or [[Tolarian Terror]]. Outrace them with lifegain.

## Elves

Tricky matchup, they can easily outscale you on board and with lifegain. Pivot to Infect + evasion.

## Ephemerate

[[Dawnbringer Cleric]] and [[Standard Bearer]] are terrible for you.

## Gardens

They have numerous sacrifice effects, focus on spamming materiel with [[Malevolent Rumble]] and [[Cartouche of Solidarity]]. [[Troublemaker Ouphe]] merits [[Tamiyo's Safekeeping]].

## Glintblade

Fight through sacrifice effects and removal. When they hit three mana, don't deploy an unbuffed creature into [[Suffocating Fumes]].

## Madness Burn

Lifegain your head above water. They will bring in [[Tithing Blade / Consuming Sepulcher]] and [[End the Festivities]] to catch you with your pants down, plan accordingly.

## Ramp

You can outrace their big threats. Beware [[Breath Weapon]] and [[Deglamer]].

## Skred

They don't have a lot to stop you, play around countermagic and [[Breath Weapon]].

## Synthesizer

Pure race. Mulligan for lifegain, play around [[End the Festivities]].

## Turbo Fog

This matchup has a horrific lategame. Kill ASAP.

## White Weenie

Race. Don't get blown out by [[Prismatic Strands]] or [[Thraben Charm]]. [[Standard Bearer]] is super painful, and your only out is pretty much [[Ram Through]].
