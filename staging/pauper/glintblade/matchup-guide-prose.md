## Affinity

Load up on artifact hate to break their mana base and keep big threats off the table. They will respond in kind with [[Gorilla Shaman]], so keep your sweepers in.

## Bogles

Load up on sacrifice effects / enchantment removal and rebuy them. You're in the driver's seat here.

## Broodscale

Make it difficult for them to deploy their combo across multiple turns with [[Tithing Blade / Consuming Sepulcher]]. Wreck their hand with [[Okiba-Gang Shinobi]] and [[Duress]]. Disrupt with [[Thraben Charm]].

## Caw-Gates

Stock up on sweepers. Youll need it to fight [[Gorilla Shaman]] and their board of weenies. They will attack your manabase with [[Dust to Dust]].

## Delver

Pressure them with discard. They will get bigger creatures faster than you, and you'll need to force through removal to keep pace with your smaller board.

## Elves

[[Arms of Hadar]] is awesome.  Consider bringing in [[Revoke Existence]] if the double value of [[Nyxborn Hydra]] is hard to deal with.

## Ephemerate

[[Tithing Blade / Consuming Sepulcher]] is pretty effective against their light creature count. Go for [[Okiba-Gang Shinobi]] when the coast is clear of removal. [[Murmuring Mystic]] is a big part of their gameplan, so it may be correct to cut something other than [[Suffocating Fumes]].

## Gardens

Squeeze the most out of your card advantage engines; they have unlimited removal and you need to keep up. Unfortunately, your engine is tied to the very creatures they're optimized to remove, so play carefuly and don't overcommit when they have open mana. [[Crypt Rats]] is also a menace. Keep fliers around at all times to discourage them grabbing Monarch or Initiative with impunity.

## Madness Burn

Side in lifegain effects to complement your robust mainboard chip gain. Their creatures are small and you have numerous fliers, so removal is not as big a priority, but [[Gorilla Shaman]] out of the board is quite dangerous. Also beware [[Trespasser's Curse]].

## Ramp

Kill [[Arbor Elf]] immediately and try to connect with [[Okiba-Gang Shinobi]] early, as they're light on removal. The late game greatly favors them due to all the Reach creatures, though you have a chance to keep up with ample removal.

## Skred

Play carefully to land a hit with [[Okiba-Gang Shinobi]], though this will be difficult. Try to keep flier pressure against their Monarch plays. Don't overextend into boardwipes.

## Synthesizer

Lifegain and sweepers, standard operating procedure. Your Inspectors make a nice meatwall against their swarms. [[Cast Down]] is better than [[Tithing Blade / Consuming Sepulcher]], but the latter is useful once you stabilize. Beware [[Gorilla Shaman]]; this merits leaving in some [[Cast Down]].

## Turbo Fog

Attack their hand aggressively with your sideboard and [[Refurbished Familiar]] loops. Flip multiple [[Tithing Blade / Consuming Sepulcher]]. They're basically a sitting duck spraying useless [[Fog]] against your discard and drain gameplan.

## White Weenie

Prune the board liberally with your sweepers. They stack a surprising amount of card advantage, so [[Okiba-Gang Shinobi]] could be merited if you can connect repeatedly.
