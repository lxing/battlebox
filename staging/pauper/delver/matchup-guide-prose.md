## Affinity

Play a little more reactively with your counters. Your 5/5s will outmuscle [[Myr Enforcer]] so racing is feasible

## Bogles

[[Annul]] and [[Deem Inferior]] for tempo. You have a small window where your serpents can outmuscle their bogles.

## Broodscale

[[Annul]] on [[Sadistic Glee]]. Use [[Brainstorm]] to protect your hand as needed from [[Duress]]. [[Deem Inferior]] is less useful since they won't combo across multiple turns for you to interact, and their board presence is otherwise nonexistent.

## Caw-Gates

You can try to go wide with [[Murmuring Mystic]]. The late game favors them as a [[Sacred Cat]] with enough [[Basilisk Gate]] support will dominate your serpents, and they have ample graveyard hate and [[Prismatic Strands]]. [[Guardian of the Guildpact]] walls you hard.

## Elves

Kill them as fast as possible before the elfball explodes. You must counter or remove [[Wellwisher]].

## Ephemerate

[[Dispel]] is strong against [[Ephemerate]] and removal, and Mystic gives you board parity. They have few ways to remove it. They will bring in [[Pyroblast]], [[Hydroblast]] accordingly.

## Gardens

A little more protection against their removal. [[Writhing Chrysalis]] is truly obnoxious and must be countered.

## Glintblade

You can try to go wide with [[Murmuring Mystic]]. Beware [[Okiba-Gang Shinobi]].

## Madness Burn

The full blast package trades efficiently for their enablers. They will bring in their own.

## Ramp

Race aggressively and counter [[Writhing Chrysalis]].

## Skred

Basically a mirror match but they have more removal, fewer snakes, no delvers. Leverage this, bring in [[Hydroblast]] to counter their [[Pyroblast]] and [[Skred]] and build a bigger board presence. You are the aggressor.

## Synthesizer

Stabilize with Blasts and serpents.

## Turbo Fog

Get in some damage, then build up a lethal strike backed by lots of countermagic. A plan B is to counter both [[Stream of Thought]] so they deck themselves. Hold on to some cantrips to rebuild from [[Relic of Progenitus]].

## White Weenie

You need to race quickly as they have quite a lot of lifegain. Use [[Murmuring Mystic]] to match their wide board, and block judiciously to not get oneshot by [[Guardians' Pledge]].
