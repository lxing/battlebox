White Weenie is board-first aggression backed by disruptive creatures. It curves out quickly, then punches above its weight in combat with protection / Shadow / tapping utility. Against combo decks, it optionally has a very light blue splash for Meddling Mage (this decklist may or may not be configured for blue).

### Early game
Mulligan for cheap creatures and get on board immediately. [[Ramosian Sergeant]], [[Savannah Lions]], and [[Mother of Runes]]; drop them and start swinging.

### Midgame
Keep attacking and make your it goal in life to turn combat miserable with awesome efficient threats. [[Ramosian Sergeant]] makes a great intermediate mana sink. Against midrange decks and creature-based combo decks, especially Dreadnought and Reanimator, dig out [[Whipcorder]] to completely lock them down. Otherwise, grab multiple [[Soltari Priest]] to rack up unblockable pressure.

### Endgame
[[Exalted Angel]] makes for a satisfying finisher when your opponents have exhausted their removal against your weenies. Use [[Cursed Scroll]] for sustained crowd control and to get in the last points of damage.
