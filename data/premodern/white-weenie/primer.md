White Weenie is pure board-first aggression backed by disruptive utility creatures. It curves out quickly, forces awkward combat with [[Mother of Runes]] and [[Whipcorder]], and closes games with evasive damage or a hard-cast [[Exalted Angel]].

### Early game
Lead on cheap creatures and get on board immediately. [[Ramosian Sergeant]], [[Savannah Lions]], and [[Mother of Runes]] give you the best starts, while [[Swords to Plowshares]] clears away the one blocker that matters.

### Midgame
Keep attacking and make combat miserable. [[Whipcorder]] taps down key blockers, [[Silver Knight]] is excellent against red decks, and [[Cursed Scroll]] turns stalled boards into inevitability. Use [[Wasteland]] aggressively when it helps preserve tempo.

### Endgame
If the early rush does not finish things, [[Exalted Angel]] usually does. Protect it, keep the board clean enough for attacks, and do not forget that even a small surviving creature can keep pressure on once your utility permanents take over.
