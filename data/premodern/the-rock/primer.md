Experience the iconic midrange trifecta of disruption, removal, and super efficient threats. Every card in the deck is built to grind down or outmuscle your opponent's resources; no tricky countermagic or flashy combos here. Well, maybe a few interesting [[Recurring Nightmare]] synergies.

### Early game
Accelerate with [[Birds of Paradise]] and [[Yavimaya Elder]] while defending with [[Wall of Blossoms]]. Trade judiciously with removal and discard; your goal is to end up with both players empty-handed, where your card-for-card quality will come out on top.

### Midgame
Enter The Rock's comfort zone. Blow [[Pernicious Deed]] to stabilize any wide boards at this point. Use [[Living Wish]] to branch into inevitability with [[Genesis]], removal with [[Bone Shredder]], or pure combat with [[Spiritmonger]]. [[Blastoderm]] and [[Ravenous Baloth]] are fast clocks that can close games out of nowhere. Continue to sprinkle in disruption as needed.

### Endgame
Finish with a nonstop stream of threats and manlands. [[Recurring Nightmare]] is an excellent endgame and chains well with [[Wall of Blossoms]], [[Living Wish]] targets, and general fatties. Your top end is dedicated to the impressive [[Spiritmonger]].
