Also referred to as "Moneyball Black", this deck doesn't have a lot of bombs, but instead plays almost like an efficient white weenie deck with more disruption.

### Early game
Your single best opening is [[Dark Ritual]] into [[Hypnotic Specter]]. Otherwise, curve out with your low curve of efficient creatures. 

### Midgame
This is where the deck snowballs. [[Ravenous Rats]] and [[Withered Wretch]] keep the board relevant while [[Smother]], [[Diabolic Edict]], and [[Snuff Out]] punish creature decks. [[Phyrexian Arena]] is your best card in longer games, so protect your life total when it is active.

### Endgame
Most games end with repeated attacks from resilient threats and manlands. [[Nantuko Shade]] is the biggest closer, but incremental attrition matters just as much. Keep trading resources until the opponent is empty-handed and your board is the only one left standing.
