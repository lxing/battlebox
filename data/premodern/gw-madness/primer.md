The more direct, aggressive counterpart to GW Madness. Spam discard synergies and swamp your opponents with a stream of efficient beaters. 

### Early game
Mulligan for a one-drop or Madness enabler. 

### Midgame
This is where the deck gets its biggest edge. Flashing in [[Arrogant Wurm]] or generating value from [[Call of the Herd]] lets you keep committing pressure without running out of material. [[Sylvan Library]] helps you find the right mix of threats, lands, and interaction, while [[Cursed Scroll]] gives you reach once both players start trading resources. Be willing to use [[Swords to Plowshares]] proactively if it clears the way for a fast clock.

### Endgame
If the game goes long, plan around [[Glory]]. Once it is in the graveyard, combat becomes heavily skewed in your favor and even modest creatures can punch through stalled boards. [[Mishra's Factory]] and [[Wasteland]] help you keep applying pressure through sweepers and mana issues, so keep attacking from multiple angles instead of assuming the deck has to win quickly.
