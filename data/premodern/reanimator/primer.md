Reanimator is a fast graveyard combo deck that trades cards in hand for absurd threats in play. [[Putrid Imp]], [[Unmask]], [[Cabal Therapy]], and [[Zombie Infestation]] stock the graveyard quickly, then [[Reanimate]], [[Exhume]], [[Animate Dead]], and the black half of [[Life / Death]] turn that setup into immediate pressure.

### Early game
Your best draws threaten a huge creature on turn 1 or turn 2. Prioritize hands that contain both an outlet and a reanimation spell, and use discard proactively to clear countermagic or graveyard hate before committing.

### Midgame
If the first reanimation attempt fails, do not panic. [[Zombie Infestation]] gives the deck a real backup engine, and repeated discard plus reanimation spells will usually find another window. Choose targets based on the matchup: [[Akroma, Angel of Wrath]] for fast racing, [[Verdant Force]] for board dominance, and [[Rorix Bladewing]] when haste matters.

### Endgame
Most games should not go long, but the deck still has staying power. Flashback on [[Cabal Therapy]] and token pressure from [[Zombie Infestation]] let you keep functioning through interaction, while sideboard creatures like [[Phantom Nishoba]] and [[Multani, Maro-Sorcerer]] let you pivot into harder-to-answer threats.
