Cycle through half your deck, turn the graveyard into mana, and finish with repeated drains from [[Drannith Stinger]] or a giant recursive threat. This is one of the least fair things in the Pauper box, but it asks for careful sequencing every turn.

### Early game
Prioritize setting up your mana and stocking the graveyard. Free or cheap cyclers like [[Street Wraith]], [[Monstrous Carabid]], [[Imposing Vantasaur]], and [[Barren Moor]] are your fuel, while [[Lotus Petal]], [[Dark Ritual]], and [[Cabal Ritual]] let you convert that setup into a combo turn very quickly.

### Midgame
Stick a payoff creature when the shields are down. [[Drannith Stinger]] is the cleanest kill, [[Drannith Healer]] buys a surprising amount of time against races, and [[Page, Loose Leaf]] helps chain through awkward hands. Once your graveyard is full, [[Songs of the Damned]] and [[Reaping the Graves]] let you storm off through a lot more interaction than opponents expect.

### Endgame
Your big turn is usually about counting mana, cycling exactly enough cards, and deciding whether to go all-in or rebuild. If the drain plan stalls, sideboard cards like [[Chitin Gravestalker]] give you a backup angle that still rewards filling the graveyard.
