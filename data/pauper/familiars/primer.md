[[Sunscape Familiar]] and [[Snap]] interact to juice a blue-white control shell into a robust combo engine. Once the cost reduction is online, cheap interaction plus card draw lets you loop creatures with [[Ephemerate]] and [[Ghostly Flicker]] until your opponent is buried.

### Early game
Setup your land drops with [[Preordain]] and [[Lórien Revealed]], and deploy [[God-Pharaoh's Faithful]] or [[Sunscape Familiar]] at first opportunity. The combo path requires a critical mass of materiel, so your opening posture is super defensive.

### Midgame
Begin chaining value creatures: [[Sea Gate Oracle]], [[Mulldrifter]] , [[Archaeomancer]]. With one Familiar in play, [[Snap]] becomes mana-positive spell, and you can start generating combo-like value chains of cards and ETB effects.

### Endgame
Take over with recursion loops. [[Ephemerate]] on [[Archaeomancer]] or [[Mulldrifter]] will usually overwhelm fair decks, and [[Ghostly Flicker]] gives you a second engine once you have enough mana. Sideboarded [[Stonehorn Dignitary]] lets you lock combat decks out entirely while [[Murmuring Mystic]] turns all the spell-chaining into an actual clock.
