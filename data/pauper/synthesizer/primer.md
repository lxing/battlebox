Aggro built around light artifact synergy and explosive token turns. Go really wide, pump things up, and overrun your opponent. Finish with burn when they stabilize on the board.

### Early game
Establish a board quickly with low‑cost creatures and artifacts. [[Voldaren Epicure]] and [[Clockwork Percussionist]] provide early pressure and artifact fodder. You MUST mulligan for a fast opening.

### Midgame
Vomit out a wide board with [[Rally at the Hornburg]] and [[Burning-Tree Emissary]]. Convert to huge alpha strikes with [[Goblin Bushwhacker]].

### Endgame
In classic red aggro fashion, you're armed with the best burn spells in the history of Magic. Take out chunks of your opponent's life 3, 7, 10 damage at a time. In protracted stalemates, [[Experimental Synthesizer]] and [[Wrenn's Resolve]] can dig out the last few points of damage from your library.
