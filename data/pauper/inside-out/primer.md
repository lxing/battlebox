Inside Out is a Boros combo-tempo deck built around [[Tireless Tribe]] and [[Inside Out]]. The deck spends the early turns filtering cards into the graveyard, then threatens a sudden kill by making [[Tireless Tribe]] huge, swapping its power and toughness, and forcing it through combat. [[Sneaky Snacker]] and [[Spirit Mascot]] give you real pressure when the combo is not immediately available.

### Early game
Set up mana and start sculpting. [[Faithless Looting]], [[Abandon Attachments]], [[Thrilling Discovery]], and [[Pursue the Past]] dig for [[Tireless Tribe]] while stocking the graveyard. Do not discard every spare card too early; extra cards in hand are the burst damage that turns [[Inside Out]] into a lethal attack.

### Midgame
Present multiple angles. [[Sneaky Snacker]] rewards the same discard engine that fuels the combo, while [[Aurora Eidolon]] and [[Sandstorm Eidolon]] come back when you cast multicolored spells and give you repeatable material for discard outlets. If the opponent is holding up removal, try to wait until you can pair the combo turn with [[Loran's Escape]], [[Prismatic Strands]], or [[Sejiri Steppe]].

### Endgame
Look for a protected attack step. [[Escape Tunnel]] can make [[Tireless Tribe]] unblockable before you pump it and cast [[Inside Out]], while [[Sandstorm Eidolon]] can remove a key blocker from combat. [[Lightning Bolt]] closes games where combat leaves the opponent low, and post-board cards like [[Flaring Pain]], [[Red Elemental Blast]], [[Shenanigans]], and [[Thraben Charm]] let you fight the specific hate or interaction that matters.
