Curve out with sticky bodies, then cash them in for huge bursts of damage. [[Carrion Feeder]], [[Bloodthrone Vampire]], and [[Mortician Beetle]] turn every expendable creature into scaling pressure, while [[Rite of Consumption]] gives the deck a clean way to convert one giant threat into a lethal swing.

### Early game
Deploy cheap creatures quickly and start forcing awkward combat. [[Nested Shambler]], [[Infestation Sage]], and [[Shambling Ghast]] all leave value behind when they die, which means your sacrifice outlets are live almost immediately. [[Supernatural Stamina]] helps you win combat or double up on death triggers.

### Midgame
Start snowballing one payoff creature. [[Carrion Feeder]] and [[Bloodthrone Vampire]] let you control combat math, and [[Mortician Beetle]] punishes both players for any sacrifice-heavy game. [[Unearth]] keeps the pressure flowing, while [[Snuff Out]] clears blockers without costing mana.

### Endgame
Look for one explosive turn. A big attacker plus [[Rite of Consumption]] can end the game on the spot, and [[Sadistic Glee]] gives you a second way to create an oversized must-answer threat. After sideboard, cards like [[Mesmeric Fiend]] and [[Tragic Slip]] let you trade some raw speed for cleaner interaction.
