Stick a protected creature, then stack dumb enchantments to end the game quickly. In postboard games, use a transformative sideboard with acceleration plus big midrange creatures to juke your opponents as they side out removal.

### Early game
Mulligan for a hexproof creature plus a couple of auras or fixing. Accelerate with [[Utopia Sprawl]] and [[Wild Growth]]. This deck basically plays itself, barring some caution for sweepers and sacrifice effects depending on your opponent's deck.

### Midgame
Stack your best auras on a creature and go to town. [[Armadillo Cloak]] will stabilize and win you any damage race. It can be quite devastating to lose your all-in creature to sacrifice-based removal, so if you know you opponent packs it, add extra fodder to the board with creatures or [[Malevolent Rumble]]. Fear no sweepers; all Pauper sweepers are damage-based and you can outgrow them.

### Endgame
Use [[Ancestral Mask]] to rip your opponent's face off.
