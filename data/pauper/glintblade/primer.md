Combine cheap ETB draw with bounce creatures to gain unlimited value. Plentiful of lifegain, card advantage, and removal form the backbone of the flagship Pauper attrition strategy. A creature base of fliers and ninjas give you numerous options to close out games. The biggest weakness is a slow start: the deck runs 8 taplands, several of which are disrupted by artifact removal.

### Early game
Develop your board with [[Thraben Inspector]] and [[Lembas]], then start looping them with [[Glint Hawk]] or [[Kor Skyfisher]]. Due to the card advantage engine, you will find yourself mana constrained, so nonstop land drops are critical.

### Midgame
Carefully balance repeated value from bouncing your value pieces against tempo and board state. Beyond [[Tithing Blade|Tithing Blade / Consuming Sepulcher]], none of your ETB effects disrupt your opponent's board, so durdling too hard for card advantage may leave you open to getting killed by faster development. Use removal freely to counteract this.

### Endgame
If you've made it this far, your material advantage should become inexorable. Chip away with impunity through flying damage while emptying your opponent's board and hand.
