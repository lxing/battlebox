A UR control deck that looks similar to Delver, but the better removal suite lets you play a more protracted and defensive control game. Thus, instead of spamming cantrips to fuel out Terrors and going aggressive with Delver, play an organic control game that lets you grab the Monarch and slowly land your serpentine finishers. This strategy is also a double-edged sword: despite being a control deck, pure card advantage in Pauper is hard to come by, and you are dependent on keeping the Monarch for sustained card advantage.

### Early game
Open with cantrips to smooth your draws and find interaction. Use [[Skred]] and [[Lightning Bolt]] to keep the board clear at instant speed in tandem with your countermagic. No rocket science here, this is control early development 101.

### Midgame
Once your graveyard is stocked, drop [[Tolarian Terror]] or a juicy [[Pirate|Azure Fleet Admiral]] [[Captain|Crimson Fleet Commodore]]. Prioritize staying ahead on board once you grab the Monarch.

### Endgame
The endgame is quite strong. [[Lórien Revealed]] is a huge card advantage lift, and you can use [[Murmuring Mystic]] to take over longer games as you start chaining cantrips.
