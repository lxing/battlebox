A UR control deck that looks similar to U Delver, but the better removal suite lets you play a more protracted and defensive control game. Thus, instead of spamming cantrips to fuel out Terrors and going aggressive with Delver, play an organic control game that lets you grab the Monarch for card advantage and land your serpentine finishers.

### Early game
Open with cantrips to smooth your draws and find interaction. Use [[Skred]] and [[Lightning Bolt]] to keep the board clear at instant speed in tandem with your countermagic. No rocket science here; this is control early development 101.

### Midgame
Once your graveyard is stocked, drop [[Tolarian Terror]] or a juicy [[Pirate|Azure Fleet Admiral]] [[Captain|Crimson Fleet Commodore]]. Prioritize staying ahead on board once you reach this turning point.

### Endgame
The endgame is quite strong. [[Lórien Revealed]] is a huge card advantage lift. [[Murmuring Mystic]] takes over longer games when you can start chaining cantrips.
