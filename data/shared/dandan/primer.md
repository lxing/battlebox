Dandân, a terribly weird format where you maneuver to get a quorum of fishes, then swallow your opponent in five bites. With 10 [[Dandân]] and 38 spells, most of the action occurs on the stack, as both players maneuver for advantage in landing and protecting their own fish for combat.

The shared library plays a big role. [[Memory Lapse]], [[Brainstorm]], [[Predict]], [[Mystical Tutor]], and [[Vision Charm]] let you manipulate the top of the library to sculpt your own draws or screw your opponent over. Each card can play double duty
