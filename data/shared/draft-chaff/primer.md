Draft Chaff is a shared-library battle box built for quick, interactive kitchen-table games with some low-mid power draft all-stars. Gameplay is super feels-good because you never get manascrewed or flooded, and there's plenty of 2-for-1s to keep the gas flowing!

### Rules
Start with 4 cards in hand and 5 basic lands + 5 allied/enemy Guildgates in the command zone. 1 mulligan to 4 allowed. Optionally, the player on the draw starts with a treasure token.

Each turn, you may play 1 land from the command zone. Draw from the shared library and keep separate graveyards.
